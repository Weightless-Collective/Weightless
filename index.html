WEIGHTLESS.
| Our Mission |

Weightless is a Colorado-based not-for-profit arts collective on a mission to become a fully recognized nonprofit. We create and host art spaces — live music experiences, community events, and creative gatherings — designed to offer refuge from the noise of modern life and foster genuine human connection.
